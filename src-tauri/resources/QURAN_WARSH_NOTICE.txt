Warsh riwayah Quran text (warshData v10) and KFGQPC Warsh font (v10)

Source: King Fahd Glorious Qur'an Printing Complex (KFGQPC), Madinah —
official developer data (https://qurancomplex.gov.sa/en/techquran/dev/).
The complete text of the Noble Qur'an in the riwayah of Warsh 'an Nafi',
in the Uthmani script of the Madinah mushaf, included verbatim without
modification (only the trailing ayah number digit is separated for
display). Verse numbering follows the Madani count used by the Warsh
mushaf and intentionally differs from the Hafs/Kufan count.
